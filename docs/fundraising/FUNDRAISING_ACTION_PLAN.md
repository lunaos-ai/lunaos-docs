## ðŸš¨ Risk Mitigation

### Fundraising Risks

**Risk**: Takes longer than 60-90 days
- **Mitigation**: Keep burn at $0, extend timeline to 6 months
- **Backup**: Consulting/services while fundraising

**Risk**: Valuation too high ($12.5M pre)
- **Mitigation**: Flexible on valuation if partner fit is excellent
- **Floor**: $10M pre (25% dilution for $2.5M)

**Risk**: No term sheets after 50 meetings
- **Mitigation**: Pivot to smaller round ($1M-$1.5M) or SAFE
- **Backup**: Revenue-based financing (Clearco, Pipe)

**Risk**: Investor wants co-investor (syndicate)
- **Mitigation**: Positive (less dilution, more expertise)
- **Action**: Help lead find co-lead
